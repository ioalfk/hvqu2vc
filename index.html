Title

HVQU2-VC: A One Shot Voice Conversion by Hierarchical Vector Quantization and Nested U-Net Structure

Description

This page presents audio demo samples of our work. The demo samples show that our proposed approach can remarkably improve the quality of the converted by combining use hierarchical vector quantization and nested U-Net structure (U2-Net) compared with other U2-Net based one-shot voice conversion models. Meanwhile, the audio samples also show that the proposed method can have the comparable performance with the TTS based one-shot voice conversion model, which named StyleTTS-VC. It has to be noted that all the models are only trained with VCTK corpus. We also use the speeches from the LibriTTS dataset to better evaluate the model performance in the unseen-to-unseen conversion scenario
